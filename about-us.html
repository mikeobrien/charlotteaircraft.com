Our Company

Charlotte Aircraft Corporation has proudly served the commercial aviation market for more than six decades. Collectively, our sales and purchasing staff have more than 10 decades worth of experience with the company alone. We take pride in staffing qualified professional problem solvers that meet the demanding needs of the industry.

Distribution

Charlotte Aircraft Corporation offers worldwide support through a close proximity to Charlotte Douglas International Airport. We utilize the services of most land, sea, and airfreight companies offering you the greatest flexibility in shipping options.

Services

At our company, we have the right personnel to assist you with any purchase. We actively maintain the ability to purchase surplus inventories of aircraft parts and engines from most major manufactures. In addition, we gladly assist organizations wanting to consign inventory.

Commitment

Charlotte Aircraft Corporation doesn't hesitate to go the extra mile. We are available 24 hours a day for any purchasing or consignment need.
